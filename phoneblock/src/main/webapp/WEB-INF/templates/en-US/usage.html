Commercial use

I advise against using PhoneBlock on a business telephone connection. If you want to do this anyway, then you bear the sole risk for any damage that may result. To minimize the risk of use, I recommend that you do not block numbers on the block list, but only redirect them to your answering machine. I also strongly advise against using the PhoneBlock answering machine in a commercial environment - under no circumstances do you want to risk having a potential customer on your telephone line "fooled" by the answering machine.
